DataOps

Author: Roy

Date: March 15, 2023

DataOps is an emerging approach to data management that focuses on collaboration and automation throughout the data lifecycle. It aims to improve the speed and efficiency of data analytics and decision-making by integrating people, processes, and tools. DataOps emphasizes the use of agile methodologies and DevOps practices to streamline the data pipeline and ensure that data is accurate, timely, and relevant. By breaking down silos and promoting cross-functional collaboration, DataOps can help organizations to achieve faster time-to-insights and improve the overall quality and reliability of their data.
